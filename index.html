About me

I am a graphic designer. I graduated from RMIT universit in Melbourne in 2018 and achieved my Master degree (Master of Communication Design). My works are including branding design, illustration, poster design, book design, creative strategy for large-scale events, KV of art exhibitions, etc. I also work as a freelance photographer. Now, I'm studying in SAIT in Calgary.
